My name is Muskan Choudhary, an enthusiastic designer with roots in fine art and narrative. As a keen admirer of aesthetics and design, I derive immense pleasure from the creative exploration of visual beauty and artistic expression. With a sharp eye for detail and a flair for invention, I am driven by a perpetual desire to create fascinating and impactful designs that speak to people on a visceral level.

Through my design portfolio, I hope to display my skills and design aesthetics. My current portfolio as a second - year design student contains works from all subject areas which includes posters, Instagram content, sketches, and content writing.

As I continue my path as a design student, I am excited to take on new challenges, broaden my skill set, and push the frontiers of creativity. Considering an open mind and an insatiable desire for knowledge, I am keen to explore new design possibilities and make a significant contribution to the world through my enthusiasm for creativity and innovation.
